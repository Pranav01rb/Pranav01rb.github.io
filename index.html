About

I build end-to-end perception pipelines for autonomous systems, specializing in LiDAR–camera fusion, 3D object detection, V2I perception, semantic segmentation, and calibration. My master’s thesis at ASU’s BELIV Lab delivered a 3D LiDAR–camera late-fusion stack with a dual-update Kalman filter that extended perception range by 252% (21.6→76m), validated through real-world testing on our research vehicle.

Projects

3D LiDAR–Camera Fusion for Extended V2I Perception

ROS 2 • Autoware • Kalman Filter • CARLA/ScenarioRunner

  • Late-fusion of onboard LiDAR with RSU camera streams for occlusion-aware 3D detection and tracking.
  • Dual-update Kalman filter for asynchronous fusion; achieved 0.51 m RMSE and 252% range extension.
  • Validated in CARLA and on-vehicle; measured latency/FPS and robustness under varied conditions.
